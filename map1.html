2020 Covid-19 Case Rate:

Hover over a state!

This map utilizes MapBox API to display the Covid-19 case rate per 1,000 people of United States counties in the 2020 year.

The data used in this map was gathered from The New York Times Covid-19 data repository and The American Community Survey's Population data. The data were additionally aggregated by Steven Bao.

The county geometries/boundaries used in this map were provided by The United States Census Bureau.

This map was made by Calvin Standaert as part of an assignment in the University of Washington's Winter 2023 Advanced Digital Geographies class (GEOG 458) taught by Professor Bo Zhao.
